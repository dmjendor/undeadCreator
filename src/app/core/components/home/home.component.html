Welcome to the Undead Creator

The undead creator was designed and created to allow necromancer characters to be able to create unique and useful undead creatures without having to create a massive army that will bog down combat and trivialize encounters with sheer numbers.

Once you have logged in/registered for the site, you will be able to create and save your undead customizations and load previously saved creations for quick reference. It will provide the "cost" of creating the undead as well as its combat statistics in an easy to read and use format.
